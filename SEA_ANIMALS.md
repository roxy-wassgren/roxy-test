# Five Remarkable Sea Animals

This document lists five notable sea animals that fit the animal theme of the project and can be reused for future content updates.

1. **Blue whale**  
   The largest animal on Earth, known for its immense size and long-distance migrations.
2. **Orca**  
   A highly intelligent marine predator that lives in coordinated family groups.
3. **Whale shark**  
   The largest fish in the world, feeding mainly on plankton despite its enormous mouth.
4. **Giant manta ray**  
   A graceful ocean giant with a broad wingspan and strong migratory behavior.
5. **Leatherback sea turtle**  
   The largest living sea turtle, adapted for long ocean journeys and deep dives.
